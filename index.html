What is this for?

You have your list of chosen sounds to assemble and combine to create words–but inevitably, you focus too much on some and not enough on others. Every language has its favored sounds, but it wouldn't do to just forget some of your carefully chosen ones.

Enter your sounds or choose an alphabet and randomly shuffle it to make sure you're considering all sounds when searching for just the right one to add.
