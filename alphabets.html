What does IPA stand for?

Several things, actually, but in this case the important ones are International Phonetic Alphabet and International Phonetic Association.
The IPAlphabet is a collection of sounds whose pronunciation and transcription is agreed upon by everyone. It allows people to study them and then know how to pronounce any word whose phonetic transcription is available.
Phonetic transcription is the visual representation of speech sounds through symbols.
